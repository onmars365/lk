公司介绍

About Us

朗坤产业园（建设中）是湖北朗坤智能科技有限公司倾力打造，一座集研发创新、智能制造、业态协同于一体的现代化综合产业园区。园区总占地21亩（约14000平方米），总投资1.2亿元，建成后可吸纳用工300余人，预计每年为地方贡献税收超千万元。园区聚焦教育用品、玩具、户外用品、宠物用品四大核心品类，深度整合研发与生产链路，具备强大的OEM/ODM定制化能力。凭借成熟的供应链体系与品质管控标准，公司已服务国内外上千家客户，业务版图覆盖全球市场。

langkun Industrial Park (under construction) is a modern integrated industrial park built by Hubei Langkun Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., integrating R&D innovation, smart manufacturing, and business collaboration. The park covers a total area of 21 acres (approximately 14,000 square meters) with a total investment of 120 million RMB. Upon completion, it will employ over 300 people and is expected to contribute over 10 million RMB in local taxes annually. The park focuses on four core categories: educational supplies, toys, outdoor products, and pet supplies, deeply integrating R&D and production chains with strong OEM/ODM customization capabilities. With a mature supply chain system and quality control standards, the company has served thousands of domestic and international customers, with business covering global markets.
